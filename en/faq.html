Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions

Getting Started

How can I join the server?

You need to own FiveM and GTA V. Search for "Capitalyx" in the FiveM server list.

Do I need an application to play?

No, the server has open access. You can connect directly and create your character.

How many characters can I create?

You can create one character per FiveM account.

Gameplay

What is a tycoon server?

A server focused on business management, jobs, and economy. You can develop your commercial empire in a relaxed atmosphere.

Can I play with my friends?

Absolutely! Cooperation and mutual aid are encouraged. You can create businesses together or trade resources.

How do I make money?

Choose a job you enjoy, develop your business, trade with other players.

Technical

The server is lagging, what to do?

Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, report it on Discord with precise details.

I lost my items/money, what to do?

Contact an administrator on Discord with evidence (screenshots, videos) and incident details.

How do I report a bug?

Use the #bug-reports channel on Discord with a detailed description and evidence if possible.

Rules and Sanctions

What are the risks of violating rules?

Depending on severity: warning, temporary kick, or permanent ban. Check the Rules page for more details.

How can I appeal a sanction?

Open a ticket on Discord in the appropriate section with your arguments and evidence.

Can I appeal a ban?

Yes, after a reflection period. Use the Discord ticket system to submit your appeal request.

Community

How do I join Discord?

The Discord link is available in-game via the /discord command or on our website.

Are there organized events?

Yes! Economic events, production contests, special markets, and community challenges are regularly organized.

How do I create an alliance/company?

You can create or join a faction/company to benefit from XP bonuses and other slight advantages that facilitate your progression.

What are the benefits of factions?

Factions mainly offer experience bonuses, slight economic advantages, and a mutual aid environment between members.

Jobs & Economy

What jobs are available?

Farmer, miner, lumberjack, merchant, transporter, and many more! Each job has its own mechanics and profit opportunities.

Can we trade resources between players?

Yes! Trading and mutual aid are at the heart of gameplay. You can sell your productions or help other players with your resources.

How does the economic system work?

The server's economy is fixed with established prices and mechanics. Focus on optimizing your productions and the efficiency of your jobs.

Need Additional Help?

  • Contact a staff member in-game
