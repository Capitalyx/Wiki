Courier / Delivery Driver

Complete guide to become a package delivery driver on Capitalyx

Where to Look

Delivery Center

Go to the delivery center to get your badge and pick up your first delivery vehicle

Location

The delivery center is located in the industrial zone, near the main warehouses. Check the map for exact location.

How to Start

1

Get the Vehicle

Activate the garage marker to spawn your delivery vehicle

2

Pick up Packages

Follow the marker to load packages into your vehicle

3

Deliver Packages

Deliver packages to the indicated addresses and collect your payment
