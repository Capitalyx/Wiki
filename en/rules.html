Server Rules

All the rules to follow for a chill and collaborative experience on Capitalyx

General Rules

1. Respect and Courtesy

Any disrespect towards other players or staff is strictly prohibited.

2. Appropriate Language

Insults, discriminatory or hateful remarks are not tolerated.

3. No Advertising

Any form of advertising for other servers or external services is prohibited.

Job Rules

4. Job Choice

Each player can freely choose their job and change it anytime according to their wishes.

5. Mutual Help Encouraged

Resource exchange and mutual assistance between players are strongly encouraged to create a supportive community.

6. Commercial Fair-play

Trades must be done in a spirit of fair-play, without scamming or exploitation.

Gameplay Rules

7. Chill Atmosphere

The server promotes a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. Avoid aggressive behavior.

8. No Griefing

Destroying or sabotaging other players' work intentionally is strictly prohibited.

Sanctions

Warning

First level of sanction for minor infractions.

Temporary Kick

Temporary exclusion from the server from 1 hour to 7 days.

Ban

Permanent exclusion for serious infractions or repeat offenses.
